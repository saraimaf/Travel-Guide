My experience in Cyprus

I recently went to Ayia Napa, Cyprus with a group of girlfriends. It was a short but very fun trip.
If you're looking for short haul then Cyprus is the place for you.

Where to stay

When I went my and my friend stayed at the Sunprime Hotel, this hotel was really good and modern.
They have a lot of options for breakfast, including a full english Each block had it's own pool, and each room was a studio so if you like cooking then I would highly recommend!
When exploring Ayia Napa we noticed that a lot of the accomodations where within walking distance and were very similar structure.
So here is a list of other accomodations you may consider:

Villas Hotels

Things to do

  • Go on a jeep tour
  • Visit Nissi Beach
  • Go to the water park
  • Rent quad bikes
  • Go scuba diving

Things to look out for

  • Lots of cats
  • If you do clubbing people are allowed to smoke indoors (not ideal for people with ashtma like me)
  • If you go on a tour make sure you wear trainers, because I didn't and there was a lot of walking
  • Bring a hat to avoid the sun in your eyes
  • If you go to Ayia Napa during off peak season a lot of things will be closed
  • Reastaurants tend to open at 4pm
  • Bring a sweater/jumper as it gets a bit chilly in the evenings
  • BRING CASH!!!
